Mission Statement

The mission of Southridge Raider Wrestling Alumni is to create a lifelong community of former Raider wrestlers, for the purposes of having increased opportunities for meaningful engagements, in order to increase awareness, pride, participation, volunteer involvement, and philanthropic commitment to the Raider Wrestling Program.


We are a 501(c)(3) organization
